### Quick Reference on Date and Time formats

| Code |Value |
|-----|-----|
|%a |Day of the week|
|%d |Day of the month (decimal number)|
|%m | Month (decimal number)|
|%b |Month (abbreviated)|
|%B |Month (full name)|
|%y |Year (2 digit)|
|%Y |Year (4 digit|
